We Are

Modular Container Home Manufacturer & Installer

Koyee Container Workshop has built a strong reputation through its successful delivery of high-quality camp accommodation and temporary facility solutions including various QATAR ENERGY and ARAMCO projects. Moving beyond conventional portacabin and flat-pack systems, Koyee has developed its own proprietary modular container building system.

Supported by our parent factory in China and local facilities in Qatar and the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, KOYEE is well positioned to meet the most demanding project requirements.

We have a proven record of delivering and installing 10,000-bed camps in just 125 days in ARAMCO and QATAR ENERGY Projects.

With a reputation built on quality, speed, and reliability, KOYEE is your trusted partner for modular infrastructure across the Middle East Region.
